## Welcome

Master the art of leveraging relational databases, specifically PostgreSQL, and gain the discernment to choose the right solution for diverse persistence needs. Upon completion, you'll be well-versed in various database types, capable of constructing robust solutions that meet customer requirements. Develop expertise in query optimization, ensure high-level data integrity through complex operations, and deepen your understanding of different data models and their practical applications.
